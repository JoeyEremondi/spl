About the Group

The software engineering research group is part of the Software Practices Laboratory.

A variety of equipment is available to support research in software engineering, including Unix workstations and PCs. A lab is currently being set-up to support the running of case studies and experiments on the use of new software engineering tools.

We hold a weekly meeting to talk about various software engineering issues. For more information, check out a web page about it and subscribe to the cs.software-eng newsgroup.
